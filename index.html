Grab a seat. The door's open for everyone — all we ask is you respect the house and the people in it.

01

Read the Room

We can't write a rule for every situation and we won't try. If something feels mean, sketchy, or out of place — don't post it. Good judgment gets you far here.

02

Kindness Is on the House

Treat people the way you'd want to be treated. No personal attacks, cheap shots, passive-aggressive jabs, or dragging people. This is a bar, not a courtroom.

Walk-Away Rule — If you're heated, step outside and cool off. Come back when you're good. Fights get you cut off.
03

No Hate. No Exceptions.

Hate speech, discriminatory "jokes," bullying, dog-whistles, targeted harassment — none of it has a tab here. Full stop. Anyone who makes someone feel unsafe for who they are gets shown the door permanently.

04

What's Said Here, Stays Here

Don't share anyone's personal information without clear consent. No doxxing, no unwanted photos, no real names or locations. Regulars trust this place. Keep it that way.

05

Debate the Idea, Not the Person

Good conversation is welcome — we love a spirited back-and-forth. Criticise ideas, not characters. Stay in the right channels, don't spam or flood, and no ear-rape pings, zalgo text, or all-caps meltdowns.

06

Keep It Clean & Legal

No porn, gore, piracy links, explicit content, or drug talk. We keep this space open to all ages and we intend to keep it that way. If you have to wonder whether it's okay — it isn't.

07

Staff Word Is Final

Mods and admins call the shots. If you're warned or cut off — take it, walk it off, move on. Arguing publicly or blowing up staff DMs only makes it worse. Respect the people keeping the lights on.

"Positive, chill, and safe for everyone."
